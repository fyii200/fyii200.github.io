I am a data scientist with research interests in common eye diseases (particularly myopia and its associated complications), drawing on insights from large-scale datasets of ophthalmic images and electronic health records. I am currently funded by an independent MRC Transition Fellowship at the University of Edinburgh and hold a visiting research position at King’s College London. 

I earned my PhD in precision medicine with integrated study in data science from the University of Edinburgh, following my undergraduate training in the Department of Vision Sciences at Glasgow Caledonian University.
