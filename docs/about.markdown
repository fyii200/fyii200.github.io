I am a data scientist with research interests in common eye diseases, particularly myopia and its associated complications. I work with large-scale imaging data and electronic health records from sources such as [SCONe](https://clinical-sciences.ed.ac.uk/ophthalmology/scone/about-scone) and the [UK Biobank](https://www.ukbiobank.ac.uk) to investigate epidemiological questions and develop computational approaches to personalised risk prediction. Following a PhD in precision medicine with Integrated Study in data science, I secured an MRC Transition Fellowship at the University of Edinburgh and hold a visiting research position at King’s College London.
